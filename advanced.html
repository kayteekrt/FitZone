Exercise Guides

Burpees

Start standing, squat down, jump feet back to plank, do a push-up, jump feet forward, and leap upward. Repeat quickly with control.

Pistol Squats

Stand on one leg, extend the other leg forward, lower into a squat keeping the lifted leg off the ground, and return to standing.

Handstand Hold / Push-Ups

Use a wall for balance. Kick into a handstand and hold with tight core. For push-ups, lower yourself toward the ground and press back up.

Jump Lunges

Start in lunge position. Jump up explosively and switch legs mid-air, landing in a lunge on the opposite side.

Plank with Shoulder Taps

In plank position, tap your left shoulder with your right hand and alternate. Keep hips level and abs engaged.

V-Ups

Lie flat, lift arms and legs simultaneously to touch above your torso, forming a 'V'. Lower back down slowly and repeat.
