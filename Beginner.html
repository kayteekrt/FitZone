Exercise Guides

Jumping Jacks

Stand tall with feet together and hands at your sides. Jump while spreading your legs and raising your arms overhead. Return to start. Repeat steadily.

Bodyweight Squats

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart. Bend your knees and lower your hips back and down, then return to standing. Keep your chest up.

Wall Sits

Lean against a wall with your feet hip-width apart. Slide down until your knees are at 90°, then hold for the desired time.

Arm Circles

Extend your arms to the sides at shoulder height. Rotate them forward in small circles, then reverse. Maintain a tight core.

Step Touches

Step side-to-side, gently tapping your opposite foot each time. Swing your arms for rhythm. Keep it low-impact and steady.

Knee Push-ups

Start on hands and knees. Lower your chest toward the ground while keeping your back straight. Push back up to starting position.
