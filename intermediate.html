Exercise Guides

Push-Ups

Start in a plank position, lower your chest to the ground keeping your body straight, then push back up. Keep elbows close for triceps focus.

Plank to Push-Up

Start in forearm plank. Push up one arm at a time into a full plank, then return to forearms. Keep hips steady.

Lunges (Alternating)

Step forward with one leg, lower hips until both knees are bent at 90°. Push back to start and switch legs.

Mountain Climbers

From a plank position, alternate driving your knees toward your chest quickly. Maintain core stability.

Bicycle Crunches

Lie on your back, bring opposite elbow to knee while alternating legs in a pedaling motion. Keep your core tight.

Glute Bridges with Leg Raise

Lie on your back, lift hips into a bridge, raise one leg up straight, then lower and switch sides. Engage glutes and core.
